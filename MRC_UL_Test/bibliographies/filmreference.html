Contains a comprehensive title index. Included are bibliographies, filmographies and many black-and-white stills.

  • Armour, Robert A.

    • Film, a reference guide / Robert A. Armour. Westport, Conn.: Greenwood Press, 1980. Series title: American popular culture.
    • For the student beginning research about film who does not know how to start. Covers American popular films from 1877 through 1979. Indexed thoroughly, each of the eleven chapters is a bibliographic essay on a particular aspect of film. Includes bibliographies. Some of sources will have since been replaced with more current titles, however the foundation for basic research is well-laid.
    • UCB Doe Refe PN1993.45 .A75
    • UCB Moff Refe PN1993.45 .A75

  • Austin, Bruce A.

    • The Film Audience: An International Bibliography of Research. (1983)
    • One of the rare reference resources where the movie screen looks back at who's sitting in the seats. This is a journal-based bibliography examining cinema and its audience. The bibliographies (indexed by author, subject, title and a periodical listing) cite material from 1930 through 1982.
    • Main Stack PN1995.9.A8A12.A9
