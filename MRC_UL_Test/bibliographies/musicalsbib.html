    • "The article discusses the feminist representation of musical films among several motion picture remakes in the U.S. It is stated that the beginning of sound era has opened a chance for the American cinema to move into a more formulaic and strategic filmmaking. This transition of musical films into the representation of feminist genre is evident on various films including "Sweethearts," "Royal Wedding," and "For Me and My Gal." These type of films also depict feminist actions such as the courtship rites, the talk about searching for the perfect partner, and even representations of sexual act. Musical films may also show gestures, postures and choreographic moves that can be associated with the social norm between men and women." [EBSCO]
